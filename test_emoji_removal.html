Alternative Error Indicators

Instead of emojis, we can use:

  • [ERROR] - Square brackets
  • ✗ Error - Checkmark cross
  • • Error - Bullet point
  • Error - Just the word (cleanest)

Success Messages

For positive states:

  • ✓ Success - Checkmark
  • • Success - Bullet point
  • Success - Just the word
